Color Film Developing Instructable


  • Why did you choose this project?
    1. I enjoy photography and I really love the feeling of taking a physical photo, developing it, and elarging it onto photographic paper.

  • What does it mean for you?
    1. It makes me feel more connected to what I'm photographing since I'm not just tapping the shutter a million times on my DSLR. The tangibility makes the photos feel real to me moreso than a digital photo. A lot of people say that color developing is harder to do at home, but it's actually easier to me than developing with black and white.

  • Where can I learn more?
    1. The Film Photography Project has a huge amount of resources and helpful blog articles and videos regarding film and other photographic processes.
